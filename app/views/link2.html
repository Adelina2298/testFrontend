Link2 2Performant

  • If you have lots of links on your website that could be turned into affiliate links but it's just too much of a hassle to change them all by hand, don't worry! Link2 can easily change them for your visitors by just adding a little piece of code on the pages they're in. All you have to do is:
  • Choose which are the destinations/programs that you want to change links for (by default, all programs are selected).
  • Choose what type of transformation you want to apply to existing links.
  • Copy the embed code to your site. We recommend placing it just above the </body> tag. You only have to do this once.
  • THAT'S IT! Enjoy having your visitors sent to the advertisers' sites with your tracking cookie already in place.
